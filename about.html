About Us

Galaxy Badminton Court is one of the mostly used and common court around Malabon City

They are open from 8:00 a.m. to 1 a.m. every day with a rate of 200 pesos per hour as a rental fee. It is called Galaxy Badminton Court because of its uniqueness as a name for a badminton court

Is the galaxy badminton court the commonly used court for some trainings?

Yes, because of where it is located. it is located inside the rivera street and less distractions inside due to the fact that it is a close court with good ventelation

What are the teams usually found training inside the court?

There are 3 teams and they are Team A, JSBC, and the Serrano Twins

Is the galaxy badminton court sponsored by any group/company

No, all of the necessities and expenses inside and outside of the court are from the pockets of the owner. in addition, Mr. Edison P. Bunyi and his wife, Mrs. Rose P. Bunyi, are the owners of the Galaxy Badminton Court. They built the Galaxy because they love and have a passion for playing badminton.
