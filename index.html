About Us

Galaxy Badminton Court is one of the mostly used and common court around Malabon City

They are open from 8:00 a.m. to 1 a.m. every day with a rate of 200 pesos per hour as a rental fee. It is called Galaxy Badminton Court because of its uniqueness as a name for a badminton court.

Trainings hosted Inside

JSBC Trainings

Coach Jesrick leads the JSBC team, which stands for Jheykez Smasher Badminton Club. Once on the team, you are already treated as a champion, and you treat
everybody on the team as a champion. The team provides 20 paid sessions at Galaxy badminton courts, with a tournament after each session to showcase the improvements made by participants.

Seranno Twins Trainings

Jonas Serrano leads the Serrano Twins, named after himself. The Serrano came from the name of the coach himself, and the Twins are how they treat each other, which corresponds to siblings and treating them equally. The team trains at Galaxy badminton
court, which is accessible to 10 years old and older. The trainings, which include 20 sessions, are paid with benefits and culminate in a tournament where participants showcase their improvements.

Team A

Amboy Deguzman leads Team A, named after the first letter in the alphabet. This team symbolizes that they are always first and on top. The training program, which includes 20 sessions and a competition, aims to improve badminton players at the Galaxy badminton court, offering benefits and opportunities for competition.
